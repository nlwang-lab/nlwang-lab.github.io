Nanlin Wang











Research Interests

  • Infrared and optical spectroscopy, time-domain THz and ultrafast pump-probe spectroscopy
  • Competing orders and collective phenomena in complex/correlated electronic materials (e.g. transition metal oxides/chalcogenides, intermetallic compounds and heavy fermions，Dirac/Weyl semimetals)
  • Unconventional superconductivity
  • Crystal growth and exploration of novel superconductors

  • NSFC funds for Outstanding Young Scientists, 2000
  • Outstanding scientific achievement award from Qiu Shi Science & Technologies Foundation, HongKong, 2009
  • YeQiSun Award of Chinese Physical Society, 2009
  • Fellow of American Physical Society, 2012
  • The first-class prize of China’s State Natural Sciences Award, 2013
  • Among the list of Thomson Reuters/Clarivate Analytics Highly Cited Researchers, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018
